Future of Payments

BC Card × EF × Visa/Mastercard

Exploring the convergence of traditional payment rails and blockchain. Deep dive into stablecoin settlement networks, interchange fee models, risk management, and merchant adoption strategies.

Infrastructure & L2 Strategy

Defining optimal Layer 2 strategies for KRW-pegged stablecoins and Real World Assets (RWA). A technical discussion on scalability, interoperability, and the Korean infrastructure landscape.

Session 03

Regulatory & STO Era

Top Securities Firms × EF (Besu)

Preparing for the 2026 STO institutionalization. Discussing hybrid architectures bridging private financial networks with public Ethereum chains using Hyperledger Besu and privacy tools.

Session 04

Institutional Asset Management

Global Asset Managers × EF

A 'toxic', no-fluff dialogue on ETH as a strategic institutional asset. Addressing valuation models, custody risks, staking yields, and the macro thesis for Ethereum in a diversified portfolio.
